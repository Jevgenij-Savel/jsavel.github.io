Cooking School

Congratulations on becoming the proud owner of a Pressure Cooker and joining our family!

If you’re not sure where to start, you’re in the right place. Our Cooking School will take you through a series of recipes, as well as some of the do’s and don’ts of pressure cooking.

Top tips to check out before you start cooking!

Within our cooking school there are three modules; beginner, intermediate and advanced. Each module includes five recipes which will allow you to use different functions and techniques, so feel free to cook as little or as many recipes as you like until you feel ready to progress to the next module.

By the time you’ve worked your way through our Cooking School you’ll be ready to experiment with your own tried and tested recipes. So what are you waiting for? Get started with our beginner module!
